Interact with Today's World (or simply ITW), it is the largest technical awareness conference organized by IEEE in Egypt. Organized by IEEE Alexandria Student Branch; ITW is a series of high-quality sessions and talks held in Bibliotheca Alexandria (BA) aiming at delivering the required up-to-date technical and nontechnical knowledge to Egypt's brightest engineering student and fresh graduates.

Monday, March 20th to Thursday, March 23rd
Bibliotheca Alexandrina
